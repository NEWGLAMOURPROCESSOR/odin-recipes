French Toast

Description

Super duper fluffy french toast that just thinking about it will make you lick your lips, yummmmm

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
    • <1i>1 pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Directions

  1. Measure flour inot a large mixin bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot
